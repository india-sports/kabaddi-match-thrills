"Bas ek touch chahiye! Ek touch!"

Kabaddi ka asli jaadu shayad isi moment mein chhupa hua hai.

Jo log kabaddi regularly dekhte hain, unke liye ye sirf ek sport nahi hai. Ye ek emotional rollercoaster hai jahan har raid ek mini-thriller film lagti hai.
